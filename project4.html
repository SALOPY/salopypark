SPEAK BY HANDS

• [2023] •
• Installation-Interaction-Graphic Design •
♥ TUTOR:SUZY CHAN ♥
●

在中国，有着一种独特的社会现状，那就是在社会中消失的残疾群体。大部分的残疾群体与社会都是隔绝的，他们不对外交流，外界也很少去主动接触他们。正如同我在初中前一直居住在一所以聋哑学生为主的特殊教育学院中，我几乎没有和这里的残障学生有所交流，因此，我想要做些什么来试着让大家与这个群体进行沟通与交流。“语言”作为群体之间沟通的最基本的工具，对于聋哑群体与听人群体来说成为了互相了解的最大的障碍 。大部分的听人群体基本不会手语，这就意味着我们失去了与聋哑群体沟通交流最直接的方式，因此，将手语普及与手语学习带入大众的视野中是十分有必要的。非听障群体尝试与听障群体进行沟通交流，能够突破社会对于正常与的定义，当我们在用同一种语言进行交流时，我们谁都不是彼此的“异类”，我们只是在平等交流的两个个体。该项目通过字体设计和出版物交互的形式，将国际手语的手势融入字体中，同时进行具有互动属性的出版物设计，能够帮助观者快速记忆手势。同时希望通过这个项目的设计，让大家愿意去接触手语，学习手语，和聋哑群体沟通、交流。

●

In China, there is a unique social situation, that is, the disabled group “disappeared” in society. Most people with disabilities are isolated from society. They do not communicate with the outside world, while the outside world rarely reaches out to them. For example, I had been living in a special education institution for deaf and mute students before junior high school. I had hardly communicated with the disabled students here. Therefore, I want to do something to make people communicate with this group.Language is the most basic tool of communication between groups. However, it has become the biggest obstacle for deaf-mute groups and non-disabled people to understand each other. Most people do not understand sign language, which means that we have lost the most direct way to communicate with the deaf-mute group. Therefore, it is very necessary to popularize sign language. When the non-hearing impaired group tries to communicate with the hearing-impaired group, it can break through the social definition of normal and abnormal. When we communicate in the same language, we are not “different” from each other, but just two sides who are communicating equally.Through font design and publication interaction, the gesture of international sign language is integrated into the font. Meanwhile, the publication design with interactive properties can help viewers to memorize the gesture quickly. Through the design of this project, I hope that everyone could get to know sign language, learn it, and communicate with the deaf-mute community with it.
