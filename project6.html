RECORDS OF HAPPY MEMORIES

☀︎ Part of my undergraduate graduation project- HAPPINESS MAPPING ☀︎
☀︎ Book Design ☀︎
♥ COLLABORATED WITH:DEQIAN ZENG;QIANXILONG PENG ♥
☀︎

记忆光谱/Records of happy memories是一本基于有关幸福回忆的调研与采访所进行编辑汇总与再编排的图文出版物。我们将整本书籍通过幸福感成因的六个维度划分成了六个章节，结合六种色彩，希望观看者能够在翻阅中感受到幸福，并思考幸福。

☀︎

Records of happy memories is a graphic publication based on research and interviews about happy memories, compiled and rearranged. We have divided the book into six chapters based on six dimensions of happiness, combined with six colours, in the hope that the viewer will feel and think about happiness as they flip through the book.
