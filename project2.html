As an emerging e-commerce platform in China in recent years, Pinduoduo has brought the piracy fervour back to the e-commerce platform once again with its rapid development. These sellers of fake goods usually attract consumers through low prices and information gaps. Visual information gaps are one of their most important tricks. This unscrupulous behaviour deeply infringes the legitimate rights and interests of consumers. Consumers inadvertently buy fake products, and often can only have themselves to blame, while the sellers can get huge economic benefits.By designing a simple webpage interactive game, the viewers can understand how the knock-off sellers deceive consumers through visual information gaps. At the same time, this project calls on everyone to resist purchasing knock-offs, and learn to legally protect their rights and interests as consumers.
